Juno Bingo

Instructions

  • Objective of the game is to match all 8 numbers to get Bingo.
  • A random number will appear and you will mark off your card by clicking, if your number matches.
  • Click next number to draw the next number.
  • Once you have all 8 numbers, then click Bingo! If you click Bingo without matching all numbers you will lose.
  • Remember you can only click Bingo once!
