Juno Bingo

Instructions

  • Objective of the game is to match 4 numbers in a row to get Bingo.
  • A random number will appear and you must mark off your card by clicking, if your number matches.
  • Click next number to draw the next number.
  • Once you have 4 numbers in a row, then click Bingo!
