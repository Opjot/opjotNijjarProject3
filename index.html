Juno Bingo

Instructions

  • Objective of the game is to match all 10 numbers before your opponent.
  • A random number will appear when you click next number.
  • You will then mark off your card by clicking, if your number is called.
  • Once you have all 10 numbers, then click Bingo! If you click Bingo without matching all numbers you will lose.
  • Remember you can only click Bingo once!
